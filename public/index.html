Welcome to the Global Day of Coderetreat! December 8th, 2012

What is it?

Global Day of Coderetreat is a world-wide event celebrating passion and software craftsmanship. Last year, over 1800 passionate software developers in 94 cities around the world spent the day practicing the craft of software development using the coderetreat format. This year, we are aiming for 200 cities! With an average of 15-20 participants per city, that's over 3000 developers! You can find out more information about the day on the 2012 global day announcement.

Where is it?

Last year, we had 94 cities around the world (last year's map). This year, we are aiming for 200! We are currently in the process of registering cities. If you are interested in hosting an event in your city. Please fill in the host form. As we register events, you can find out if there is one in your city on the official GDCR2012 event list page. As we get official events registered, we'll be adding to the map below. We currently have more than 50 cities expressing interest.

Where will Corey be?

In order to raise awareness, Corey Haines will repeat his time-traveling feat from last year. He will facilitate the first coderetreat in Sydney, Australia, hop a flight across the International Date Line and land in Honolulu, Hawaii, in the morning. He'll then facilitate the last coderetreat of the day.
